Raptor.

The complete logo system. One accent, one element: the dot. Wordmark, "R." monogram for tight spaces, and the gem rank-crest. All Signal cyan — no second hue.

01 · Primary Wordmark

The word, with the cyan dot.

03 · Rank Mark · Gem Crest

The crest is a separate, earned mark.

The brand logo (above) and the rank identity are deliberately different things. The rank mark is the gem crest defined in the 8-tier rank-crests system — used on the rank-up moment, athlete profile, and leaderboard. It is never a substitute for the "R." app icon, and the app icon is never a rank crest.

04 · Specs & Rules

Clear space, minimum size, color.

Clear space
≥ 1× cap height
No element may enter the box defined by the height of the capital "R" on any side.
Minimum size · wordmark
22px
Below 22px the dot loses visibility. Use the "R." monogram instead.
Minimum size · monogram
16px
The "R." holds down to favicon scale; the dot stays attached, never floats.
Accent color rule
Signal #3FD9C0 · dot only
On light, the dot deepens to #1A8A78 (same cyan family). Never a different hue, never on a letter.
05 · Do & Don't

Usage rules.

Do
Raptor.
Keep Signal cyan on the dot only, on dark backgrounds.
Don't
Raptor.
Don't move the accent onto a letter. The dot is the only accented element.
Do
R.
Use "R." when space is tight — favicon, app icon, notification badge.
Don't
Raptor.
Don't recolor the dot. Only Signal cyan (#3FD9C0) — never lime or any other hue.
Do
Raptor.
On light, deepen the dot to #1A8A78 for contrast — same family, not a new color.
Don't
Raptor.
Don't gradient-fill the wordmark, italicize, condense, or change the weight. Geist 900 only.
