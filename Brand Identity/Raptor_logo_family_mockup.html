04 · Specs & Rules

Clear space, minimum size, color rules.

Clear space
≥ 1× cap height
No element may enter the bounding box defined by the height of the capital "R" on any side.
Minimum size · wordmark
22px
Below 22px the dot accent loses visibility. Use monogram instead.
Minimum size · monogram
24px
Below 24px the rank bars disappear. Use plain "R" without bars.
Accent color rule
Signal #3FD9C0 on dark · #65A30D on light
The accent dot/letter adapts to background. Never use #3FD9C0 on white — fails WCAG.
05 · Do & Don't

Usage rules.

Do
Raptor.
Use the full wordmark with Signal cyan accent dot on dark backgrounds.
Don't
Raptor.
Don't change the accent color. Only Signal cyan (or lime on light) is allowed.
Do
Ra
Use the "Ra" monogram when space is tight (favicons, app icon, notification badge).
Don't
Raptor.
Don't italicize, condense, stretch, or change the weight. Geist 900 only.
Do
Raptor.
Switch accent to #65A30D lime on light backgrounds for WCAG contrast.
Don't
Raptor.
Don't gradient-fill the wordmark. The accent is a single solid color, always.
