02 · Design Details

Per-tier anatomy.

Bronze

First log · Entry tier
Simple shield, no ornaments, no wings. Brushed bronze gradient. Recessed center with no gem — feels heavy, entry-level. Every athlete starts here.
Gradient: #E0A878 → #B07A42 → #6E4A22
Gem: Off (dark recess)
Wings: None
Horns: None
Glow: 0%

Silver

3 goals · 50% adherence
Shield with slight wing accents at top. Polished silver gradient. Faint center glow in Mist gray — the first hint of a gem forming.
Gradient: #D8DEE6 → #9AA3AF → #5C636E
Gem: Faint gray glow
Wings: Accents only
Horns: None
Glow: 10%

Gold

7 goals · 75% adherence
Shield with upward-pointing horns. Warm gold gradient. Center gem activates in Signal cyan at low intensity. The first tier that feels "earned".
Gradient: #FFE08A → #E5B83C → #9A7615
Gem: Signal cyan · 50%
Wings: Accents
Horns: Upward, short
Glow: 25%

Platinum

12 goals · 80% adherence
Shield with full wings spreading outward. Cool platinum gradient. Center gem at medium intensity. The "ascending" tier — wings signal momentum.
Gradient: #FFFFFF → #CFE6F0 → #8FB4C4
Gem: Signal cyan · 60%
Wings: Full spread
Horns: None
Glow: 35%

Diamond

20 goals · 85% adherence
Faceted geometric crest — not shield, angular. Cobalt gradient with visible internal facet lines. Center gem in full cobalt at high intensity. The "crystalline" tier.
Gradient: #AEC0FF → #6B8AFF → #3A52C4
Gem: Signal cyan · 70%
Wings: None (faceted)
Horns: None (angular)
Glow: 45%

Master

30 goals · 90% adherence
Ornate crest with horns + wings + base pedestal. Deep purple gradient. Center gem pulses with Signal cyan glow. The "rare" tier — fewer than 5% of athletes reach it.
Gradient: #CBB8FF → #9B7BF5 → #5E3FB0
Gem: Signal cyan · 80%
Wings: Full
Horns: Dual
Glow: 55%

Grandmaster

Top 500 regional
Highly ornate — extended wings, dual horns, ornate base pedestal. Warm gold-purple gradient. Center gem in full Signal cyan with strong glow. The "this is rare" tier.
Gradient: #FF96A6 → #E0556B → #9C2435
Gem: Signal cyan · 90%
Wings: Extended
Horns: Dual, large
Glow: 70%

Apex

Top 100 regional
The most ornate crest. Full wing span, triple horns, pedestal with tier name engraved. Onyx metal base with Signal cyan gem at maximum glow. The gem appears to emit light. Only 100 athletes in the region hold this at any time.
Gradient: #9BFFEE → #3FD9C0 → #1A8A78
Gem: Signal cyan · 100% (glowing)
Wings: Full span
Horns: Triple
Glow: 100%

04 · Division Pips

I · II · III within each tier.

Each tier has three internal divisions. Shown as small pips at the base of the crest, not as separate badges. A Diamond III athlete has 1 filled pip; Diamond I has 3 filled pips. This gives 24 total rank positions before Apex.
