Community Guidelines
Respect the community.
Raptor ranks athletes and coaches by measurable data — not by who shouts loudest. Keep the community focused on progress.
Core Rules
01
No harassment
Insulting, harassing, or body-shaming another user gets you permanently banned. No warnings. No appeals. This applies to DMs, public comments, and reported messages.
02
No fake data
Uploading falsified InBody scans, edited progress photos, or fabricated body metrics to climb the leaderboard is permanent ban + legal action. Raptor verifies scans via OCR cross-checking.
03
No off-platform coaching
Coaches who bypass Find-a-Coach commissions or pull clients off-platform to dodge fees lose their account. See Find-a-Coach anti-abuse policy in Privacy.
Zero-tolerance policy
Permanent ban + legal action
Insulting or harassing someone will get you permanently banned. Raptor supports legal action against severe harassment, threats, or defamation. Reported messages are reviewed within 24 hours. False reports are also a violation.
Moderation consent
Heavy auto-moderation
On by default. Scans messages for flagged words and harassment patterns. Only metadata stored — not full message text.
Coach + athlete consent to disable. If both agree to keep banter between them, moderation lifts for that conversation only.
